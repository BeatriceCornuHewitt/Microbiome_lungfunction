## Study Overview
The scripts in this repository are related to a study assessing the associations between the upper respiraotry tract (URT) microbiome (nasopharyngeal (NP) and oropharyngeal (OP)) to spirometry results, both cross-sectionally and longitudinally over a follow up time of 7 years
All participant data comes from the Dutch VGO studies (https://www.rivm.nl/veehouderij-en-gezondheid/onderzoek-veehouderij-en-gezondheid-omwonenden-vgo).
